How did I start my Photography Business?

I started my photography business a year ago. It only started as a hobby, taking photos for my family and friends, as well as capturing every activity at my university. However, I soon found myself wanting to capture many moments and memories. But then I realized that other people also deserve their moments to be captured, so I aim to create a memory bank or a collection of their moments taken by my camera for them to have something to look back on.
